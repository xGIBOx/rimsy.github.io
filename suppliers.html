Meet our partners who are at the forefront of making and designing rims for all types of vehicles. Visit their official websites and get acquainted with all the ins and outs of making rims.

BBS

BBS Kraftfahrzeugtechnik AG is a high-performance automobile wheel design company headquartered in Schiltach, Germany. BBS produces wheels for motorsport, OEM, and aftermarket applications.

KANSEI

Kansei was created by wheel enthusiasts with a passion for design, simplicity and spirited driving. We build performance wheels that complement the heritage of the classics while utilizing modern engineering, materials and construction. Our curated line of wheels were designed for the spirited driver and their nostalgic taste.

KONIG

KÖNIG Wheels is currently one of the most recognizable aftermarket wheel brands in the world. For over 40 years Konig has set their standards high and watched enthusiasts from all over the world embrace not only their wheel styles but also the lifestyle that comes along with them.

AODHAN

Aodhan is a company out of Hayward, California with more than 10+ years of experience in the wheel design game. The name Aodhan means born of fire and is pronounced odd-han. Their mission is to improve affordable wheels without sacrificing quality.
